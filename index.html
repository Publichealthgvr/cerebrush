The world’s first behavior-first smart toothbrush

How It Works

1. Pick up the brush
2. Hear your personalized audio cue
3. Build a lasting brushing habit

Why It Works

Tracking alone isn’t enough. CereBrush turns brushing into a feel-good ritual using sound, science, and routine.

Designed For

  • Wellness-focused morning routines
  • People with ADHD or cognitive fatigue
  • Children, seniors, and anyone struggling with brushing

The Tech Behind It

Built with an audio module (DFPlayer Mini), triggered by movement. Plays affirmations or cues. Recharges via USB. Designed for grime-free docking.
