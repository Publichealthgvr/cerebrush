Why CereBrush?

CereBrush isn’t about tracking. It’s about building habit. Using audio cues at the moment of action, we turn brushing into a wellness ritual — not a chore.

How It Works

  • Pick up the brush
  • Hear a personalized affirmation or sound
  • Brush better, longer — without needing to think about it

Designed For

Wellness lovers, ADHD warriors, neurodiverse thinkers, kids, elders — anyone who struggles with brushing consistently.
